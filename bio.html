Short Career History

Senior Research Officer, May 2010 - May 2012

University of Essex

I was working as a research officer on the AutoAdapt project, which is a GBP 287K EPRSC project that aims to develop and evaluate methods for adapting automatically constructed domain models to the population of users' search or browsing behaviour.

KTP Associate, May 2008 - May 2010

Acitve Web Solutions ltd. & University of Essex

A joint project funded under the Government Technology Strategy Board scheme with a total funding of GBP 127K. The aim of the project is to develop an innovative technique to support collaboration in cross-enterprise projects. This involves analysing emails exchanged between project stakeholders and applying natural language processing and semantic search technologies to help users in finding and browsing information.
