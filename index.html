Research Interests

I specialise in Information Retrieval (IR) in general. My major research interest is using data-driven approaches for providing users with information access methods in new environments other than the web, which include news media, enterprises and smart urban environments (smart cities). Here is a list of the topics I have worked/am working on:

  • News Information Retrieval
  • Entity Linking and entity-based retrieval
  • Large Scale Crowdsourcing
  • Urban Big Data and Information Access in Smart Cities
  • Social Media Retrieval and Sensor data retrieval
  • Context-aware Query Suggestions
  • Enterprise Email Search
